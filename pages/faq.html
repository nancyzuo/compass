Eligibility and who are we looking for?
申請資格與我們在尋找什麼樣的學員？

The Compass Fellowship is for curious and ambitious university students interested in reflecting and exploring greater breadth and depth in understanding themselves and the world.

We accept university students (or those of age 18-23), particularly for those in Taiwan, accepting about 20 students for each cohort.

While the program won’t have any hard pre-requisites, the staff team tends to have careers in STEM, so comfort with mathematics is recommended, but not a requirement.

Compass Fellowship 誠摯邀請懷有好奇心與影響力抱負的青年，一起踏上反思、探索及改變自己與世界的旅程。

本營隊主要招募 18–23 歲青年，尤其歡迎在台灣的學生。每梯次約錄取約 20 位學員。

營隊未設 GPA、科系等硬性條件，但多數講師具有STEM背景；若不畏懼數學將會對你有所幫助，但並非必要條件。

How does the admission process work?
申請流程如何進行？

Our admissions will be run in two stages. A written application, followed by an interview. Our interviews are ~45 minutes and are meant to be conversational with one of our staff, we want to see you as you are and how you normally think. Admissions will be running from mid-November to early January on a rolling basis. This means we will start interviewing as soon as applications are reviewed, usually within one to two weeks, until spots are filled. If the application is still open, feel free to send in your application as last minute admits are still possible. Application officially closes on January 3, 2026.

營隊錄取流程分為「書面申請」與「面試」兩階段。 面試時間約45分鐘，將由一位營隊講師以輕鬆對話的方式進行，希望能認識你平常的樣貌，以及是如何在日常中進行思考。若對自身英文程度有所顧慮，可以選擇中文面試，但我們仍會進行一些基本的英文能力考核，以確保課程的語言難度不會太高。錄取作業將從11中旬開始，採隨到隨審的方式進行，並於2026/1/3（六）截止。當審閱申請資料後，約一至兩週內會安排面試，直至錄取人數額滿為止。只要申請系統仍開放，歡迎你送出申請，即使接近截止日提交，我們仍可能進行最後階段的錄取。

Why are you running this program?
為什麼要舉辦這個課程？

The program is inspired by programs like SPARC, which has been operating since 2012 with alumni that has gone on to be prominent researchers, high-valuation founders, and much more. We think the ability to model the world, introspect on one’s own experience, and implement one’s vision are all crucial, yet often neglected, skills in one’s education. There is much room for expressing interesting and effective means of honing these skills and we are particularly interested in how this could work in an East Asian context. Having already had positive experience in prototyping education programs in Taiwan, we are excited to pilot a program that is more deliberately Asian focused.

本營隊深受 SPARC 啟發。自 2012 年起運作至今，SPARC 已培育出許多在各領域表現卓越的校友，無論是學術研究（如Harvard, OpenAI, Google Deepmind等)、創業 (如ScaleAI, 0xPARC等)，或其他影響世界的實踐者。我們相信「理解世界」、「自我覺察」以及「實現願景」的能力，都是教育中至關重要、卻常被忽略的核心素養。特別在東亞文化脈絡下，這些能力更具有獨特的培養方式與發展潛力。 2024 與 2025 年間，我們在台灣已累積正向的教育實驗經驗，也因此更加期待此次專為台灣及周邊地區青年設計的課程。

Will this program be run in English?
這個 Compass 營會用中文進行嗎？

The program will be run in English. Though most staff members are proficient in Chinese. It is recommended that you are comfortable in an English speaking environment.

That said, if you are interested in applying, we strongly encourage you to do so, even if you might not be sure your English isn’t up to par! We are looking for participants with high willingness to participate, not perfect English abilities.

本營隊將以英文進行，但多數講師皆能用中文基本交流。

我們建議申請者能對身處英語環境感到自在。然而，只要你對課程有興趣，即使不確定自己的英文程度是否足夠，我們仍非常鼓勵你提出申請！我們尋找的是願意投入、積極參與的學員，並非英語能力完美的人。
