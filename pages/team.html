David Yu

David is doing research in scalable collaborative cultures – how we can design better ecosystems for humanity to solve some of the largest social-technical problems facing us today. In the past, he has spent 5 years in tech startups as a director of growth at one, and an anthropologist at another. He runs SPARC in the US and helped run two camps in Taiwan during 2024 and 2025. He is interested in complexity theory, organizational theory, and philosophy. David is currently learning to assemble his own watches.

David 正在研究「可擴展的協作文化」: 如何打造更良好的社會生態系統來面對當今重大的協調問題。過去，他在科技新創領域工作了五年，期間曾擔任一家公司成長總監，並在另一家公司擔任人類學研究員。他在美國主導 SPARC，並於 2024 與 2025 年協助在台灣舉辦兩場營隊。他的興趣包含複雜性理論、組織理論與哲學。目前，David 正在學習自己組裝手錶。
